Why this shape

Pages-first deployment

The site follows the same public shape as the `duckcells` Pages setup: a polished root entry point for humans and a dedicated app mounted under a stable subpath.

WASM where it matters

The Rust parser stays authoritative. The browser shell is just orchestration, storage, and presentation around the generated WebAssembly package.

Honest browser constraints

Arbitrary page fetching is limited by site CORS policies on static hosting. The app surfaces that cleanly instead of pretending the browser can bypass it.
